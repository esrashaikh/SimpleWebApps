FAQs

What types of memberships does WorkFitGo offer?

we offer a range of membership options tailored to your needs:
1)Monthly Membership: Provides flexibility for newcomers or those preferring short-term commitment.
2)Quarterly Membership: Offers a balance between commitment and flexibility.
3)Annual Membership: Ideal for dedicated individuals seeking value and benefits.

What facilities are available at WorkFitGo?

Discover the exceptional facilities at WorkFitGo designed to cater to your fitness needs.
Step into our Cardio Zone, equipped with cutting-edge machines for enhanced fitness.
Unleash your strength in our dedicated Strength featuring free weights and resistance machines.
Experience the synergy of community and motivation in our Group Classes led by expert instructors.

Can I get personalized fitness guidance?

Absolutely! At WorkFitGo, our certified trainers provide personalized one-on-one sessions
tailored to your fitness goals. With expert guidance and motivation, you can
achieve exceptional results on your fitness journey.

How do I sign up for a membership?

Signing up for a membership at WorkFitGo is hassle-free. You have two convenient options:
online and in-person. Online, simply visit WorkFitGo.com, pick your desired membership plan
(Monthly, Quarterly, or Annual), and complete the secure registration process.
If you prefer a personal touch, come visit our gym, where our staff will guide you through the selection
and registration process in person. We've made joining WorkFitGo easy, so you can focus on your fitness journey with us.
